About

I'm interested in Theoretical Computer Science. My focus is on algorithms and data structures, particularly in the context of graph theory. I've gained hands-on experience through projects in the field of succinct data structures (in particular minimal perfect hash functions) and studying tools and heuristics for Graph Isomorphism testing. Right now, I'm working in the Laboratory for Web Algorithmics (LAW) at the University of Milan.

I'm currently pursuing a Master's degree in Computer Science at the University of Milan, where I also completed my Bachelor's studies. I am also a student at Orthogonal School.
